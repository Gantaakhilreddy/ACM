About VVITU-ACM

The Association for Computing Machinery (ACM) at Vasireddy Venkatadri International Technological University (VVITU) is a vibrant student community dedicated to advancing computing as a science and profession.

Our chapter organizes regular technical events, workshops, and competitions to help students enhance their skills, network with professionals, and stay updated with the latest trends in technology.

Every Friday, we host engaging sessions ranging from coding challenges to expert talks. Our annual technical fest "Spardha" is the highlight of the year, featuring hackathons, quizzes, and innovation challenges.
